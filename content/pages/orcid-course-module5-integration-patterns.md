## Integration Patterns

Integration works best when teams choose a practical pattern and implement in manageable steps.

### Common Patterns

- CRIS-driven synchronization.
- Repository profile enrichment.
- Publisher or grant platform linkage.

### Selection Criteria

Prioritize impact, technical feasibility, and team capacity.
