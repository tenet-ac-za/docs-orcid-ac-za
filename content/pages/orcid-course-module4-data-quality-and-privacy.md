## Data Quality and Privacy

Good ORCID support balances high-quality metadata with researcher control.

### Quality Checks

- Are affiliations current and clearly described?
- Are works and funding links complete and accurate?
- Are duplicate or outdated entries removed?

### Privacy Checks

Review visibility settings and confirm user intent for public, limited, or private items.
