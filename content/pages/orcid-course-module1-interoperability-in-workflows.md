## Interoperability Across Research Workflows

ORCID helps systems exchange consistent researcher information instead of re-entering profile data in every platform.

### Interoperability in Practice

ORCID integrates with:

- CRIS systems
- Institutional repositories
- Publisher platforms
- Grant and funding workflows

When ORCID is connected, researcher information can remain consistent across services and reduce duplicate data entry.

### Why This Matters for Libraries

Libraries often support multiple systems that all need trustworthy author and contributor metadata.

Using ORCID as a shared identifier improves data alignment and reduces support overhead.

## Mini Activity

Map one researcher journey at your institution (submission, repository deposit, reporting). Mark where ORCID can reduce friction.
