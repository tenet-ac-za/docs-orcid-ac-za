## Support Playbook

Create a repeatable ORCID support routine for your library or research office.

### Include in Your Playbook

- Standard onboarding steps.
- Common issue resolutions.
- Escalation path for technical or policy cases.

### Team Outcome

A shared ORCID support process that improves consistency and response quality.
