## Support Channels and FAQs

Documented support channels and FAQ answers reduce repeated queries and speed up onboarding.

### Recommended Channels

- Short how-to guides.
- Service desk templates.
- Drop-in support sessions.

### FAQ Topics

- Linking records.
- Privacy controls.
- Correcting metadata.
