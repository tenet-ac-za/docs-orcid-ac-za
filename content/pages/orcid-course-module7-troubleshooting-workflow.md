## Troubleshooting Workflow

A standard workflow helps support teams resolve ORCID issues consistently.

### Suggested Flow

1. Classify issue type.
2. Confirm affected system and data source.
3. Apply known fix steps.
4. Escalate with clear evidence when needed.

### Practice

Test the flow using two sample support tickets.
