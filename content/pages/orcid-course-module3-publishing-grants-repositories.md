## Publishing, Grants, and Repositories

ORCID is most powerful where identity data moves between systems.

### Typical Touchpoints

- Manuscript and publisher submission systems.
- Grant and funding application processes.
- Institutional repository deposits and profile pages.

### Practical Benefit

Consistent ORCID use improves attribution and reduces repeated data entry.
