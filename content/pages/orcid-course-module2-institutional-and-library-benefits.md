## Institutional and Library Benefits

ORCID improves data quality and reduces duplication across institutional workflows.

### Institutional Value

- More reliable reporting and analytics.
- Better alignment of records across systems.
- Stronger evidence for strategic planning and compliance.

### Library Value

- Better metadata trust in repository and discovery systems.
- Fewer manual cleanup tasks for author identity matching.
- Improved support services for researcher profiling.

Where does identity mismatch create the most support burden in your current workflows?
