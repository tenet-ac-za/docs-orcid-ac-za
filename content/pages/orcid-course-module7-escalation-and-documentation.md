## Escalation and Documentation

Clear escalation and documentation practices make support more reliable over time.

### What to Document

- Issue summary and reproduction context.
- Steps attempted and outcomes.
- Final resolution and owner.

### Why It Matters

Documentation builds institutional knowledge and reduces repeated troubleshooting.
