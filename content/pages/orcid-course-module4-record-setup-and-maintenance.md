## Record Setup and Maintenance

Librarians can help researchers establish complete and useful ORCID records.

### Setup Essentials

- Confirm ORCID account basics and preferred display name.
- Add affiliation and key research outputs.
- Review visibility settings for intended sharing.

### Maintenance Habit

Encourage periodic updates to keep records accurate and current.
