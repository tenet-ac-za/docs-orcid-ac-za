## Local Case Exercise

Apply ORCID practices to one real institutional scenario.

### Exercise

1. Select one current researcher workflow.
2. Identify a point where ORCID can improve data quality.
3. Propose one implementation step for the next 30 days.

### Debrief

Share findings across teams and decide ownership for follow-up actions.
