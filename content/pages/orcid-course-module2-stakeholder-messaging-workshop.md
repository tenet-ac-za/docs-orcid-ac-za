## Stakeholder Messaging Workshop

Use a simple messaging map to communicate ORCID value clearly to different groups.

### Workshop Steps

1. List your stakeholder groups.
2. Define one high-value ORCID benefit per group.
3. Capture the most likely concern each group will raise.
4. Draft a clear one-sentence response.

### Output

A short messaging sheet your team can reuse in training and support.
