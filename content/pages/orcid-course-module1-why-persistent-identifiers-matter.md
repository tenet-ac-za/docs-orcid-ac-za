## Why Persistent Identifiers Matter

Persistent identifiers solve key trust and attribution challenges in scholarly communication.

### Name Ambiguity

Common names can cause confusion in citations, reports, and analytics.

For example, many authors may appear as "Wang, Y" in databases. ORCID eliminates this ambiguity by uniquely identifying individuals.

### Trust in Metadata

ORCID records can link to verified affiliations, funding, and research outputs.

This improves confidence in institutional reporting and makes scholarly metadata more reliable.

### Better Attribution Over Time

An ORCID iD keeps attribution stable across career changes, institutional moves, and naming variations.
