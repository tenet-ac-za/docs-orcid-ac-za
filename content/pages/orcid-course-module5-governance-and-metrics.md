## Governance and Metrics

Sustainable ORCID integration requires clear governance and measurable outcomes.

### Governance Questions

- Who owns ORCID-related policy and support?
- How are updates communicated across teams?
- What is the escalation path for unresolved issues?

### Metrics to Track

- Linked ORCID records.
- Metadata quality improvements.
- Support ticket volume and resolution time.
