## Benefits for Researchers

ORCID helps researchers keep a consistent identity across publishers, funders, repositories, and institutional systems.

### Core Benefits

- Reduces confusion caused by common names.
- Improves attribution across publications, datasets, and grants.
- Supports career mobility when researchers move institutions.

### Practical Impact

Researchers spend less time re-entering profile information and more time on their work.
