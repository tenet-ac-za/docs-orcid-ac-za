## Implementation Planning

Use a short-term implementation plan to move from discussion to execution.

### Plan Components

- Target system and workflow.
- Responsible owners and support roles.
- Timeline with check-in milestones.
- Risks and mitigation actions.

### Deliverable

A 30-day plan with clear ownership.
