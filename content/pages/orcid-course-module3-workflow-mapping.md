## Workflow Mapping

Map a local end-to-end researcher workflow and identify where ORCID can remove friction.

### Mapping Prompts

- Where is researcher identity currently entered?
- Where is identity data copied between systems?
- Where does mismatch or duplication occur?

### Result

A shortlist of integration and process improvements.
