## Case Study Analysis

Use case studies to identify implementation patterns that can be reused locally.

### Analysis Questions

- What problem was addressed?
- Which ORCID integration or support step solved it?
- What constraints or enablers affected success?

### Output

A shortlist of practices to pilot in your institution.
