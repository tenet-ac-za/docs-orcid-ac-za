## Adoption Campaign

Run a focused campaign to increase ORCID engagement in a measurable way.

### Campaign Plan

1. Define a target group.
2. Choose communication channels.
3. Set a success metric.
4. Run for a fixed short period.

### Review

Evaluate outcomes and refine support materials.
